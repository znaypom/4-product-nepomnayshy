Junior Development Program

2018/2019 Junior Development Program

Cannon Mountain’s Junior Development Program is designed for skiers and snowboarders who visit on a regular schedule. The program offers a progression, for skiers and riders age 3 – 15 years old, looking to develop their skills on all types of terrain and to maximize the recreational experience. Our dedicated instructors work diligently to create a fun and safe learning atmosphere that cultivates age appropriate social and athletic growth while providing individualized attention within a structured group format to foster a lifelong love of snowsports.

By: Jimmy James

Woman's Ski Group

Women's Ski Group - $35 per session

Based around the schedule of moms with children in the race & seasonal programs. No kids in the programs? That's fine! Drop-ins are also welcome at any time! Program runs every Saturday from December 30 until the last Saturday in March. There is also a Tuesday women's group which runs from 9:45 - 11:45am. It will emphasize skill building from one week to the next, though drop-ins are encouraged and welcomed.

Your instructor Randy Trainor is a Level 3 certified instructor and has taught skiing in Colorado, New Zealand, and Austria and at Attitash and Loon Mountains. She was a race coach for the Franconia Ski Club for 7 years and has been teaching the women's clinics here at Cannon for the past 6 years. Helping Randy is Donna Kaye Erwin, also a Level 3 certified instructor.

By: Jimmy James

Men's Ski Group

Men's Ski Program - $35 per session

The men’s seasonal program will focus on giving men an affordable opportunity to explore Cannon’s terrain and will focus on improving your skiing skills to obtain optimal mountain experience. This program will run this Saturday, December 16 and then on SATURDAYS starting January 6 through March 3 at 9:45am -11:45am and will emphasize skill building from one week to the next, though drop-ins are encouraged and welcomed.

Your Instructor, LT (Leighton Terwilliger), is a PSIA Level 3 certified instructor with over 28 years of experience. The class enjoys the same instructor each week and will focus on improving your skiing skills so that you will advance your skiing ability to ski all of Cannon Mountain by the end of the season. We will also tackle some topics to improve your skills for mogul skiing, tree skiing, crud skiing, powder skiing and spring skiing. Intermediates and advanced skiers are welcome to join. If you want to improve your skiing, this is the place!

By: Jimmy James
